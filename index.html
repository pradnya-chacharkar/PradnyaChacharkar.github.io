About Me

Having started my career eight years ago as a System Engineer with Infosys, the mixed feeling of thrill, joy and content achieved by a successful release still remains the same. Starting off as a software developer, I have an extensive experience with System and Solutions Design, Development, Testing, Release and Change Management. As I worked across diverse domains like telecommunications and financial services, I realised the need for understanding and utilising data to it's fullest. That's when I decided to pursue Masters in Information Systems.

It's important that the product is designed considering the customer demands and firm's IT strategy. And, it's equally important to convey the product's true potential to the end user. Today, the process does not stop at building super awesome products, but only after the end user has fully appreciated it's value. I strive to apply my technical and leadership experience, delivering the most efficient solutions.
